# Shoulder

  • Rhomboid pulls x14

    • Difficulty: Beginner

    1. Keeping both arms extended while you raise your arms in an forward motion
    2. Squeeze the upper back muscles and hold for a second.
    3. Lower your arms back to the starting position.
    4. Pause, then push yourself back up and repeat.

  • Side arm raise 16sec

    1. Keep feet pointed straight ahead.
    2. Raise both arms, to the side of the body to shoulder level.
    3. Return arms slowly back to the side of the body and repeat.

  • Side lying floor stretch 30sec

    1. Start by laying on your side, keeping your one knee slightly bent in front of you and the other extended out behind the bent.
    2. Extend your arms out over your head, hands on top of each other, reaching out above you.
    3. Hold this position for 15 to 30 seconds and then release.
    4. Repeat for as many reps and duration as desired.

  • Cat cow pose 30sec

    1. Begin on your hands and knees in table pose, with a neutral spine. As you inhale and move into cow pose, lift your sit bones upward, press your chest forward and allow your belly to sink.
    2. Lift your head, relax your shoulders away from your ears, and gaze straight ahead.
    3. As you exhale, come into cat pose while rounding your spine outward, tucking in your tailbone, and drawing your pubic bone forward.
    4. Release your head toward the floor — just don’t force your chin to your chest. Most importantly, just relax.

  • Rectilinear rhomboid squeezes x12

    1. Sit on the floore with your knees bent and your arms extended in front of you at shoulder height.
    2. Slightly lean your upper body back . Pull your elbows back and squeez your shoulder bazes.
    3. Return and repeat.
